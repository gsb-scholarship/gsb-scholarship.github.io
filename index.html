Purpose

This list is maintained to help students of GSB community get in touch with scholarship providers.

General Process

You can send a postcard to the mentioned address with your details like :

  1. Name, Address, Contact Number, School, Class

  2. Mention need for scholarship - Merit / Financial difficulty

  3. Request for application

Notes

  • The list published is not verified, I am just a aggregator of this information not a scholarship provider.

  • For quick clarification of queries, I have created a telegram channel . Join GSB Scholarship Information Telegram Channel
